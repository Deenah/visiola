Our mission

The Visola exist to support the emergence of a new cadre of African leaders by mentoring and training acadamically excellent youth in the Science, Technology, Engineering Mathematics(STEM) fields. Students pursuing careers in the STEM fields have better employment opportunities, as their skills are critically required to stimulate Africa's growing industry and manufacturing base. We aim to support an increasing number of ethical female female entreprenurial leaders with cutting-edge technical skills to lead Africa's long-term socio-economic transformation.

Vision

Visiola's vision centers on promoting values- based training, long-term planning, effective partnership, accountability, innovation and bold leadership in STEM fields.
